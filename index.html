从翻译硕士到 AI Builder

From Translator to AI Builder

我的本科和硕士都学的是英语翻译。毕业后在新东方做留学文书指导，之后去了广州外国语学校当升学顾问。这些工作教会我一件事：把复杂的信息翻译成对方能理解的语言——无论对方是学生、家长还是招生官。

2025 年初，我第一次接触 Cursor，发现这种「翻译能力」在 AI 时代有了全新的用武之地——我可以把业务需求精确地「翻译」给 AI，让它帮我把想法变成真正能用的产品。2026 年接触 Claude Code 之后，我的开发效率和交付能力再次跃升。

从那以后，我从零搭建了 5 个 AI 项目：情感记忆系统 Remoire、AI 角色扮演聊天平台、求职工具包 CareerForge、小红书图文生成器、法语学习网站。每一个项目都是从一个真实的需求出发，靠 Vibe Coding 一步步做出来的。

我不是科班出身的程序员，但我知道怎么把一个想法变成一个能用的东西。这就是我的超能力。

My undergraduate and master's degrees were both in English Translation. After graduation, I worked as a study-abroad advisor at New Oriental, then as a college counselor at Guangzhou Foreign Language School. These roles taught me one core skill: translating complex information into language anyone can understand.

In early 2025, I discovered Cursor and realized this "translation ability" had an entirely new application in the AI era. I could precisely translate business requirements for AI, turning ideas into real, functional products. After picking up Claude Code in 2026, my development efficiency leaped to another level.

Since then, I've built 5 AI projects from scratch: Remoire (an emotional memory system), an AI roleplay chat platform, CareerForge (a job-seeking toolkit), a Xiaohongshu content generator, and a French learning site. Every project started from a real need and was built step by step through Vibe Coding.

I'm not a trained programmer — but I know how to turn an idea into something that works. That's my superpower.
